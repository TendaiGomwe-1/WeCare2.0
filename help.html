About WeCare.org

WeCare.org is an initiative to help reduce substance abuse in Zimbabwe. This has been necessitated by the realization that substance abuse has been on the rise, especially among the youth. This has largely been attributed to the current economic difficulties obtaining, unemployment among other factors. At WeCare, we felt it necessary to come up with a platform to help reduce the incidence of having people being hooked onto drugs. We also found out that there was a general increase in the number of single mothers owing to, in large part, divorce and also, the HIV/ AIDS scourge.

The main thrust of this initiative is to be able to discerminate information and helpful tips about how people can avoid substance abuse, and if they are already hooked, we help facilitate their rehabilitation by linking them with professional institions and practitioners who will help them through the rehabilitation processes. This also applies to the single mothers, who we also help with information on how best they can take care of their children, even in the absence of a spouse. We also help with information with organisations where they can find help and networking with others. WeCare also intents to fundraise for children with single parents so that they can continue with their education as well as the basic neccesities of life, which includes, but not limited to food and healthcare. Are You A Single Mother?

Are you a divorced, widowed or just a single parenting mother? While it can be a very tough challenge trying to raise up a family alone without the help of a spouse, in a single parented family, it should not necessarily be. Here at WeCare.org we believe that single parenting can actually be more simpler if single mothers have all the right information. We have useful tips that can be helpful to reduce the burden on you. We also provide you with the neccessary information about other organizations and link you with facilities where you can get help. You are not alone, help is just a click away!

Substance Abuse Problems?

Substance abuse is a very big problem especially among the youth. Do you have problems with drugs? Or do you know someone who is abusing drugs? There are many helpful ideas of how to avoid being hooked on drugs for those who are not yet doing so. Even better, we have useful information, tried and tested that has proved to help people hooked on drugs to stop the habit!
