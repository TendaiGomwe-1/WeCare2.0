Are You A Single Mother?

Being a single mother can be a very daunting task. The first step is to acknowledge that having to raise kids as a single parent is not ideal. However there are a few things that you can do to get started on the right path. One of those things is, "TO GET INFORMATION"!

Whatever you do just know that you are not alone and we are here to help in any way possible!

Here are a few hints to get you started!

Tips For Single Mothers

  • Dont rush to judge your child-because they are kids they will always make mistakes, you can help by not always being prejudgemental on them.
  • Trust yourself and trust your child-there is always that fear that you may fail to raise a child the right way. Relax! Trust that you and your child will just turn out just fine.
  • Learn about children- learn about how to raise kids, attend workshops, read books,get as much information as possible.
  • Dont be tough to your child- while it may be good to be strict, chidren need gentleness and patience
  • Try not to stereotype your child- (especially boys) can be naughty. Just because they are expected to behave a certain way does not necessarily mean your son will do the same.
  • Keep asking questions- asking questions will make you wiser.
  • Spend time with your child- the more time you spend with your child the more you understand what they are going through and the better the bond
  • Dont badmouth your child's father- nomatter how bad their father were, do not say anything to paint him as such.
  • Find your child a role model-find your child a role model so that he can learn from him.
  • Establish respect-if you respect your child they will give it back.
  • Teach your child the Values you expect-take time to instruct your child on what you expect of them.
  • When they are grown-ups, let them go-give them the space when they are grown up
  • Demonstrate to them good examples-children are naturally good observers demonstrate with exaples they can see.
  • Accept that your child is different-your child is different, dont compare them to anyone else.
  • Your son is not your husband-dont put too much responsibilities on him because he's not your husband
  • Show that you love your child-children need to be treated with love and they are easy to raise where they feel loved.
