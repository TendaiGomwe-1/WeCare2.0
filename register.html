WeCare.org Substance Abuse Survivor's Forum

  1. WeCare.org has launched a secure, private online support forum for substance abuse survivors and single mothers to receive help or share their experiences.
  2. If you know someone who may need support with drug abuse and parenting, be free to use any other of our various services on this site.
  3. This forum is FREE and there are NO fees or dues of any kind.
  4. The forum is a very supportive, caring, understanding, and loving environment in which substance abuse survivors, single mothers and those on rehabilitation can reach out for help, help others, socialize, and establish relationships.
  5. We take time to research and engage expert advice on this Forum. We work with countless substance abuse survivors and some of the top online forum experts in the world to establish a safe, secure, and comfortable environment with extensive survivor materials
  6. The posts ARE NOT accessible to the public, and no part of the forum is available to search engines, so your privacy is guaranteed.
  7. To ensure the safety of our forum, membership is by application only, but we strongly encourage you to register if you are a survivor or a single mother
  8. Applications from substance abuse survivors as young as 14, and all single mothers are accepted but those who are 14-17 must receive permission from a parent or guardian before applying or becoming a member.
  9. Therefore, whether you have lost a loved one to substance recently or decades ago, we would love to welcome you into the WeCare.org Forum family.

How To Apply/ Register

To apply for membership to the forum, please provide the following information. All of the information is required. Failure to complete the application in entirety may result in the rejection of your application.

Please provide as much information as possible. DO NOT worry about writing too much. And please complete ALL areas of the application. Thank you.

Please number your answers or simply cut and paste the questions into your e-mail client and type in each answer after each question.

Please e-mail the completed application towecare@gmail.com with “Request to Join Forum” in the subject line.

You must answer all questions completely.

Thank you very much!

  1. Your full name.
  2. Your gender (male or female).
  3. Your complete home address (Your address WILL BE confidential)
  4. Your age (you must be 14 or older).
  5. Your Date of Birth (month, day, and year).
  6. Phone number. (Your phone number WILL BE confidential)
  7. Some information about the substance abuse(s) you endured. Share as much information as you are comfortable sharing, but, at a minimum, please let us know who you lost and some basic information about what happened OR for single mothers: information about the cahllenges you face(d) and how you solved them( if you did).
  8. Tell me a little about yourself. Feel free to share anything you feel comfortable sharing about things such as your work, hobbies, family, goals, struggles, etc. Again, share only what you are comfortable sharing, but please let us get to know you a little better.
  9. Reason(s) why you would like to join the forum.
  10. Anything else you would want to share with us(about anything).
  11. How did you find out about the WeCare.org Forum?

Please e-mail the completed application to wecare@gmail.com with “Request to Join Forum” in the subject line. (Please ONLY use this e-mail address for issues associated with joining the forum.)

Thank you and God bless you.

We look forward to hearing from you. We are here to help.
